About the Statue of Unity

The Statue of Unity is a monument dedicated to Indian independence movement leader Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, who was the first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister of independent India.
The statue is located on a river island facing the Narmada Dam in the state of Gujarat, India. It is the world's tallest statue, with a height of 182 metres (597 ft).
